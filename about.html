About Us

Procurement intelligence built by practitioners, for practitioners.

Our Story

Manu Forti Intelligence was founded with a clear mission: to give procurement leaders the data and insights they need to make confident supplier decisions. After years of navigating complex supplier evaluations and category strategies in the energy sector, we saw a gap in the market for fast, rigorous, and actionable procurement intelligence.

We combine deep industry expertise with advanced research capabilities to deliver supplier analysis and category strategies that actually move the needle. No fluff, no generic reports — just the intelligence you need to negotiate better, mitigate risk, and build resilient supply chains.

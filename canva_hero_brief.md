## Canva Pro Upgrade Specifications

### Canvas Size
- **Dimensions:** 1920 x 1080 px (16:9, full-width hero)
- **Safe Zone:** Keep text within center 1200px for readability

### Color Palette (Brand Consistent)
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Navy | #002147 | Primary background, headers |
| Cobalt | #2B6CB0 | Accents, CTA highlights |
| White | #FFFFFF | Text, cards |
| Steel Gray | #718096 | Secondary text |
| Light Gray | #F7FAFC | Section backgrounds |

### Design Elements to Include

#### 1. Background Layer
- **Base:** Navy gradient (existing)
- **Overlay:** Subtle geometric pattern or abstract network lines (low opacity 10-15%)
- **Accent:** Cobalt blue glow/blur effect on right side (visual interest)

#### 2. Main Visual (Right Side)
**Option A - Product Mockup:**
- 3D-style rendering of Product 1 report (9-slide deck)
- Slight tilt/perspective for depth
- Subtle shadow underneath

**Option B - Abstract Illustration:**
- Procurement/analytics themed illustration
- Connected nodes representing supply chain
- Navy + Cobalt color scheme

**Option C - Dashboard Preview:**
- Screenshot of Category Strategy Excel template
- Blurred/simplified for background use
- Overlay gradient to blend with navy

#### 3. Typography
| Element | Font | Size | Weight | Color |
|---------|------|------|--------|-------|
| Headline | Inter/SF Pro | 72px | Bold | White |
| Subhead | Inter/SF Pro | 24px | Regular | White 90% |
| CTA Text | Inter/SF Pro | 18px | Semi-bold | White |

#### 4. Trust Indicators (Below CTA)
- Small text: "Trusted by procurement teams at..."
- 3-4 company logos (placeholder/generic)
- Or: "€3,999 Category Strategy | €99-149 Reports"

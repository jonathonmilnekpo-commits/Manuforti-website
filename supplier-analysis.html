Most procurement decisions are made with incomplete information. Our supplier analysis reports give you the full picture — financial stability, risk exposure, competitive position, and ESG credentials — in a format you can present to leadership immediately.

Every supplier evaluation involves risk. Will they deliver? Are they financially stable? What happens if they fail? These questions keep procurement leaders awake at night — especially when supplier failures can halt billion-dollar projects.

Manu Forti combines deep research with professional presentation. We analyse financial statements, credit ratings, market position, news sentiment, and ESG performance. The output is a 9-slide deck that answers the questions your CFO and CPO will ask.

Use it for pre-qualification, final due diligence, or annual supplier reviews. The format follows industry intelligence standards — professional, presentation-ready, and designed for executive consumption.

What You Get

Early warning for supplier risk.
Competitive intelligence. Reputation protection.

Supplier failures don't happen overnight. They start with missed deliveries, executive departures, credit downgrades, ESG incidents — signals that appear in the news weeks or months before the crisis hits.

Most procurement teams learn about supplier problems too late. A critical component fails. A key supplier enters administration. An ESG scandal breaks. By the time you hear about it, your options are limited and expensive.

Manu Forti's Media Monitoring gives you the early warning system you need. We continuously track your suppliers across news, trade publications, social media, and regulatory filings. When something significant happens — financial distress, leadership changes, cyber incidents, contract wins or losses — you know immediately.

Use it for supplier risk management, competitive intelligence, ESG monitoring, or market awareness. The Monitor tier gives you a weekly digest of all coverage. The Alert tier adds real-time notifications for critical events. Enterprise adds custom sources, stakeholder tracking, and analyst commentary.

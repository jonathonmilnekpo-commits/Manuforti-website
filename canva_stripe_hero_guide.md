## STEP-BY-STEP DESIGN

### STEP 1: Background
1. Click background (anywhere on canvas)
2. Click color swatch → "Document colors"
3. Enter: `#0A0E1A`
4. **Result:** Deep navy-black background

### STEP 2: Gradient Glow Effect (Right Side)
1. **Elements** → search "circle"
2. Select solid circle shape
3. Resize to **800 x 800 px**
4. Position: X: 1400, Y: 540 (right side, centered vertically)
5. **Color:** Gradient
   - Click circle → "Color" → "Gradient"
   - Color 1: `#3B82F6` (Electric Blue)
   - Color 2: `#06B6D4` (Cyan)
   - Angle: 45°
6. **Blur effect:**
   - Click "Effects" → "Blur"
   - Set to **80-100** (heavy blur for glow)
7. **Opacity:** 40-50%
8. **Result:** Soft blue-cyan glow on right side

### STEP 3: 3D Abstract Shape (Center-Right)
1. **Elements** → search "3D"
2. Filter: "Graphics" → look for:
   - Crystal/gem shapes
   - Geometric orbs
   - Abstract spheres
3. **Recommended options:**
   - "3D crystal"
   - "3D sphere gradient"
   - "Abstract 3D shape"
4. Select one with blue/cyan coloring (or closest match)
5. **Resize:** 400-500 px width
6. **Position:** X: 1200, Y: 450 (center-right)
7. **If color doesn't match:**
   - Click shape → "Edit image" → "Adjust"
   - Tint: Blue +20
   - Temperature: Cool -20
8. **Add glow behind shape:**
   - Duplicate the shape
   - Make larger (600 px)
   - Apply blur effect (60)
   - Send to back (behind main shape)
   - Opacity: 30%
9. **Result:** Floating 3D element with depth

### STEP 4: Headline Text
1. **Text** → "Add a heading"
2. **Type:** 
   ```
   Take a Grip of
   Your Procurement
   ```
3. **Font:** Inter
4. **Size:** 72 pt
5. **Weight:** Extra Bold (800)
6. **Color:** White `#FFFFFF`
7. **Line spacing:** 0.9 (tight)
8. **Letter spacing:** -2 (slightly tight)
9. **Position:** 
   - X: 200 (left side)
   - Y: 350 (upper-middle)
10. **Width:** 700 px (constrain text block)

### STEP 5: Subhead Text
1. **Text** → "Add a subheading"
2. **Type:**
   ```
   Supplier intelligence and category strategy 
   for procurement teams who demand clarity.
   ```
3. **Font:** Inter
4. **Size:** 24 pt
5. **Weight:** Regular (400)
6. **Color:** Gray-300 `#D1D5DB`
7. **Line spacing:** 1.4
8. **Position:**
   - X: 200
   - Y: 550 (below headline)
9. **Width:** 600 px

### STEP 6: Primary CTA Button
1. **Elements** → search "rectangle"
2. Select rounded rectangle
3. **Resize:** 200 x 56 px
4. **Color:** Gradient
   - Color 1: `#3B82F6` (Electric Blue)
   - Color 2: `#06B6D4` (Cyan)
   - Angle: 90° (top to bottom)
5. **Corner radius:** 8 px
6. **Position:**
   - X: 200
   - Y: 650
7. **Add text:**
   - Click button → "Add text"
   - Type: "Explore Solutions"
   - Font: Inter, 16 pt, Semi-bold (600)
   - Color: White
   - Center in button

### STEP 7: Secondary CTA (Text Link)
1. **Text** → "Add a little bit of body text"
2. **Type:** "View Sample Report →"
3. **Font:** Inter, 16 pt, Medium (500)
4. **Color:** Gray-300 `#D1D5DB`
5. **Position:** 
   - X: 430 (right of primary CTA)
   - Y: 665 (vertically centered with button)

### STEP 8: Trust Bar (Bottom)
1. **Elements** → search "line"
2. Add horizontal line
3. **Width:** 1200 px
4. **Height:** 1 px
5. **Color:** Gray-500 `#6B7280`
6. **Opacity:** 30%
7. **Position:** 
   - X: 360 (centered)
   - Y: 850
8. **Add text above line:**
   - "From €99 supplier reports to €3,999 category strategy engagements"
   - Font: Inter, 14 pt, Medium
   - Color: Gray-500 `#6B7280`
   - Center aligned
